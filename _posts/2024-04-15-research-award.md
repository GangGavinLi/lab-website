Dr. Gang Li has been selected to receive the **2024 Junior Faculty Outstanding Research Award** from the James W. Bagley College of Engineering at Mississippi State University. This prestigious award recognizes exceptional research accomplishments by early-career faculty members.

The award acknowledges Dr. Li's groundbreaking work in AI-enabled predictive maintenance for renewable energy systems and his development of novel transmission technologies for tidal energy converters. His research has attracted significant external funding, including grants from the National Science Foundation and the Department of Energy.

"This recognition reflects the hard work and dedication of our entire research team," said Dr. Li. "We're committed to advancing sustainable energy technologies and training the next generation of engineers to address global energy challenges."

The award will be presented at the College of Engineering's annual awards ceremony in April 2025.

Congratulations to Dr. Li and the entire research team!
